The process

Simple from start to finish

01

Send us your designs

Upload your artwork or email it to us. We accept PNG, PDF, AI, SVG, EPS and CDR. Not sure about placement or sizing? We will help figure it out.

02

We send you a mockup

Before we print anything, we send you a mockup showing exactly how your shirt will look. Front, back, sleeve — you approve it, then we get to work.

03

Pick up your order

Your design is printed onto DTF film and heat pressed directly onto your shirt. Permanent, vibrant and wash-safe. Pick up in Brampton, GTA delivery available.
